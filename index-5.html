Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1st May 2026

Photo Shoot Models (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled safely, securely, and in accordance with UK data protection laws, including the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website www.photoshootmodels.co.uk and our related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Social media links
  • Modelling details (measurements, experience, portfolio information)

Account & Profile Information

  • Photos you upload
  • Your e‑folio content
  • Booking details
  • Messages or enquiries sent through our website

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Cookies and usage data
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Photo Shoot Models account
  • Build and publish your e‑folio
  • Provide your same‑day e‑folio site after your shoot
  • Process bookings and studio appointments
  • Improve our website and services
  • Communicate with you about updates, bookings, or support
  • Maintain safety, security, and fraud prevention
  • Comply with legal obligations

We never sell your personal information.

3. Your E‑Folio

When you create an account or complete a studio session, your e‑folio is created and published on the same day as your shoot. Your images, details, and portfolio information may be visible to brands, agencies, casting directors, and other industry professionals. You control what information appears on your e‑folio and may request edits or removal at any time.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website performance, analyse visitor behaviour, remember your preferences, and support login and account features. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Professional photographers, makeup artists, and studio staff involved in your booking
  • Trusted service providers (hosting, email services, analytics)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

We do not share your information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

6. How We Store and Protect Your Data

We store your data securely using encrypted servers, secure data centres, access controls, and regular security monitoring. We keep your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, you can contact us at:
